Product Changelog — Play the game

March 1, 2026
Improved Urgent Need Indicators
  • Feed and Repair buttons now pulse with a golden glow when the corresponding need drops below 60%
  • Glow only appears when you can afford the action, serving as a clear call-to-action
March 1, 2026
New Enlightenment Win Condition
  • Three-tier enlightenment progression: Awakening (10k karma), Bodhi (50k karma), and Nirvana (200k karma + all soul upgrades maxed)
  • Mandala gains glowing rings as you reach each tier — blue, violet, then white
  • Milestone celebration popups with particle bursts when reaching Awakening and Bodhi
  • Nirvana Challenge: survive 5 minutes with no material upgrades to prove your soul's resilience
  • Victory screen with lifetime stats upon achieving Nirvana
  • Death during the Nirvana trial fails the challenge, but you can retry
March 1, 2026
Fixed Rebirth at Zero Karma
  • Players can now rebirth even when karma is exactly zero
  • Previously, negative karma per second with zero karma made it impossible to rebirth
March 1, 2026
Balance Improved Faster Gameplay & Visual Juice
  • Karma base rate increased 5x (0.1 → 0.5/s) and wealth rate tripled (1 → 3/s)
  • Hunger and shelter drain faster, creating more active decision-making
  • Life events now trigger every 2–5 seconds instead of 3–8
  • Number popups are larger with scaling based on value, black outlines, and pop-in animation
  • Mandala now has a breathing glow that brightens with karma rate
  • Particle bursts fire on Feed, Repair, Rebirth, and upgrades with multiple color variants
  • Mandala pulses on button actions for tactile feedback
February 28, 2026
New Unified Layout & Visual Redesign
  • Combined Game and Upgrades into a single scrollable view with tabbed sections
  • Higher contrast design with more vibrant colors throughout
  • Clearer visual distinction between purchased, affordable, and unaffordable upgrades
February 28, 2026
New Auto-Feed & Auto-Repair
  • New soul upgrades: Auto Feed and Auto Repair
  • Once unlocked, toggle switches appear to enable/disable automatic need management
  • Auto-actions trigger when the need drops below threshold and you have enough wealth
February 28, 2026
New Prestige Soul Upgrades
  • Spend karma across lives to unlock permanent soul upgrades
  • Enlightened Mind, Resilient Body, Worldly Fortune, and Meditation
  • Upgrades persist through rebirth and death
February 28, 2026
New Life Events & Material Upgrades
  • Random life events that affect karma, wealth, hunger, and shelter
  • Material upgrades create a Faustian bargain: wealth and comfort at the cost of karma drain
  • 6 material upgrades with escalating tradeoffs (Fine Clothing, Ornate Home, Feast, etc.)
February 28, 2026
New Initial Release
  • Core game loop: earn karma through virtuous living across the cycle of birth and rebirth
  • Needs system: hunger, shelter, and health must be managed to survive
  • Wealth generation and spending on Feed and Repair actions
  • Death and rebirth mechanics with karma multiplier progression
  • Animated mandala visualization that responds to game state
  • Particle effects and floating number popups
